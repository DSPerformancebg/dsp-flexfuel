Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

DSP FlexFuel Monitor does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

DSP FlexFuel Monitor does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your local device and communicates only with your DSP FlexFuel Bluetooth sensor.

2. Bluetooth & Location

The app requires Bluetooth permission to scan for and connect to your DSP FlexFuel BLE sensor. On Android devices below version 12, the operating system requires Location permission to perform Bluetooth scans — this is a system requirement by Google and is not used by the app to determine your actual location.

No location data is ever recorded, transmitted, or stored.

3. Data Storage

The app stores the following preferences locally on your device only:

• Gauge color preferences
• Language selection (English / Bulgarian)
• BLE device name

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

4. Third-Party Services

DSP FlexFuel Monitor does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third parties.

5. Internet Access

The app does not connect to the internet. All communication is exclusively via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) between your phone and the DSP FlexFuel sensor hardware.

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
